## Logging in to Your 5paisa Account

After successfully downloading the 5paisa app, it's time to log in to your account. Here's how you can do it:

1. Locate the 5paisa app icon on your device's home screen and tap on it to open the app.
2. On the app's login screen, you will find two options: "Login" and "Register." Since you already have an account, tap on the "Login" button.
3. Enter your registered mobile number and the password associated with your 5paisa account in the respective fields.

Yes, you can download the 5paisa app on multiple devices using the same login credentials. This allows you to access your account from different devices, ensuring convenience and flexibility.

- On the login screen, tap on the "Forgot Password" option.
- Enter your registered mobile number.
- You will receive an OTP (One-Time Password) on your registered mobile number.
- Enter the OTP in the designated field.
- Create a new password for your 5paisa account.
- Confirm the new password.
- Tap on the "Reset Password" button.
- Your password will be successfully reset, and you can now log in using the new password.

### 3. How can I ensure the security of my 5paisa account?

- Be cautious while using public Wi-Fi networks and ensure you're on a secure connection before logging in to your account.
