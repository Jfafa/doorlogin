Before we begin, make sure you have the 1Tap Receipts app installed on your device. You can find the app on both the Apple App Store and Google Play Store. Search for "1Tap Receipts" and install the app on your compatible device.

### Logging in to 1Tap Receipts

Once you have successfully installed the app, follow the steps below to log in to the 1Tap Receipts app:

1. Launch the 1Tap Receipts app by tapping on its icon on your device's home screen.
2. On the login screen, enter your registered email address and password in the corresponding fields.
3. After entering the required credentials, tap on the "Login" button to proceed.

### Troubleshooting Login Issues

Encountering login issues can be frustrating but don't worry, we've got you covered. Here are some common login issues you might face along with their solutions:

#### 1. Forgot Password

If you forget your password, follow these steps to reset it:

- On the login screen of the 1Tap Receipts app, tap on the "Forgot Password" link.
- Enter the email address associated with your 1Tap Receipts account.
- Check your email inbox for a password reset link.
- Click on the link provided and follow the on-screen instructions to set a new password for your account.
- Once the password reset is successful, go back to the login screen and log in with your new password.

#### 2. Account Locked

If your account is locked, follow these steps to regain access:

- Contact 1Tap Receipts' customer support via email or phone to inform them about your locked account.
- Provide them with the necessary details to verify your identity and ownership of the account.
- Once your identity is verified, the support team will unlock your account.
- You can then go back to the login screen and log in as usual.

#### 3. Unable to Receive Password Reset Email

If you are not receiving the password reset email, follow these steps:

- Double-check the email address you entered for a typo or error.
- If the email address is correct, ensure that the email hasn't landed in your spam or junk folder.
- Add "noreply@1tapreceipts.com" to your email contacts or whitelist to avoid future email delivery issues.
- If you still don't receive the password reset email, contact 1Tap Receipts' customer support for further assistance.

By following the troubleshooting steps above, you should be able to overcome most login issues encountered while using the 1Tap Receipts app.
