# How to 3HK WiFi Auto Login

In this article, we will discuss in detail the process of automatically logging into 3HK WiFi networks. 3HK is a popular telecommunications company operating in Hong Kong which offers WiFi services to its customers. By automating the login process, you can save time and energy, especially when you frequently connect to 3HK WiFi networks. Let's delve into the methods for 3HK WiFi auto login.

## Method 1: Utilizing 3HK Mobile App

The 3HK mobile app provides an easy solution for auto logins on their WiFi networks. Follow the steps below to configure auto login through the app:

1. Install the latest version of the 3HK mobile app from your app store.
2. Launch the app and log into your 3HK account using your credentials.
3. Once logged in, navigate to the settings or preferences section of the app.
4. Look for an option related to WiFi auto login or network preferences.
5. Enable the auto login feature if it is available and follow any additional instructions provided within the app.
6. Save your settings and exit the app.

By utilizing the 3HK mobile app, you can conveniently automate the WiFi login process.

## Method 2: Configuring Auto Login on Devices

Apart from using the 3HK app, you can also configure auto login settings on your individual devices. This method ensures that your devices automatically connect to 3HK WiFi networks without any manual intervention. Below, we have outlined the general steps to achieve this on commonly used devices:

### On Android:

1. Access the Settings app on your Android device.
2. Locate and tap on the "Wi-Fi" option.
3. Find the 3HK WiFi network you usually connect to and tap on it.
4. Next, tap on the "Advanced" option.
5. Within the advanced settings, look for an option called "Auto Connect" or "Auto Login". Enable it.
6. Your Android device will now automatically connect to the 3HK WiFi network when it is in range.

### On iOS:

1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone or iPad.
2. Tap on "Wi-Fi" and select the 3HK WiFi network you usually utilize.
3. Tap the "i" icon next to the network name.
4. Look for the "Auto-Join" option and enable it.
5. Your iOS device will now automatically connect to the 3HK WiFi network when available.

Configuring auto login on your devices ensures seamless and hassle-free connectivity to 3HK WiFi networks.
