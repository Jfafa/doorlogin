1 Lakota is an online portal designed to streamline the communication and educational processes within the Lakota Local School District. From checking grades and attendance to accessing learning resources, the 1 Lakota portal serves as a central hub for all things Lakota.

To begin the login process, follow these steps:

2. Enter the following URL in the address bar: 1lakota.learning.powerschool.com.
3. You will be redirected to the 1 Lakota login page.

### Section 3: Logging in as a Student

If you are a student, here's what you need to do:

1. On the login page, locate the "Sign In" section.
2. Enter your Lakota student username and password in the provided fields.
3. Once you have entered the correct credentials, click on the "Sign In" button.
4. Congratulations! You have successfully logged in to the 1 Lakota portal as a student.

### Section 4: Logging in as a Parent

As a parent, you can monitor your child's progress and engage with their educational journey through the 1 Lakota portal. To log in as a parent, follow these instructions:

1. On the login page, look for the "Parent Sign In" section.
2. Enter your PowerSchool ID and password in the respective fields. If you don't have a PowerSchool ID, contact the Lakota school administration for assistance.
3. Once your credentials are entered correctly, click on the "Sign In" button.
4. Voila! You are now successfully logged in to 1 Lakota as a parent.

### Section 5: Logging in as a Staff Member

For Lakota staff members, the login process is slightly different. Here's how you can access the portal:

1. On the login page, find the "Sign In to PowerSchool SIS" section.
2. Enter your username and password provided by the Lakota administration.
3. After inputting your credentials, click on the "Sign In" button.
4. Great job! You are now logged in to the 1 Lakota platform as a staff member.
