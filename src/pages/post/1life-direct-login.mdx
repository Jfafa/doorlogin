Yes, you can easily reset your 1Life Direct password if you forget it. Follow the steps below:

- Visit the official 1Life Direct website.
- Click on the "Login" button.
- Below the login form, click on the "Forgot Password?" link.
- Enter your registered email address in the provided field.
- Click on the "Reset Password" button.
- You will receive an email with instructions on how to reset your password.
- Follow the instructions in the email to successfully reset your 1Life Direct password.

#### 2. What should I do if I encounter issues logging into my 1Life Direct account?

If you are experiencing difficulties logging into your 1Life Direct account, try the following troubleshooting steps:

- Double-check your login credentials for accuracy.
- Ensure that the Caps Lock key is off, as passwords are case-sensitive.
- Clear your browser cache and cookies.
- Try using a different web browser, as the issue might be browser-related.
- If the problem persists, contact 1Life Direct customer support for further assistance.

#### 3. Are there any mobile apps available for 1Life Direct login?

Yes, 1Life Direct provides mobile apps for both Android and iOS devices, offering a convenient way to access your account on the go. To download the app:

- Search for "1Life Direct" in the app store search bar.
- Locate the official 1Life Direct app and click on it.
- Tap the "Install" or "Get" button to download and install the app on your device.
