To begin the login process, you need to visit the official website of 360 Promotions. Open your preferred web browser and enter the URL in the address bar. Upon reaching the login page, you will be presented with a login form. Here, you need to input your credentials to gain access to your 360 Promotions account. Fill in the required fields with your registered email address or username and your corresponding password. Take care to enter your details accurately to ensure a successful login.

In case you have forgotten your password, fear not! 360 Promotions provides a simple password recovery process. To initiate this process, on the login page, locate and click on the "Forgot Password" link or button. Once your request is submitted, 360 Promotions will send an email containing further instructions to reset your password. Keep an eye on your inbox and follow the provided steps to generate a new password. Remember to choose a strong, unique password to enhance the security of your account.

1. **Incorrect Credentials:** Double-check the information you have entered and ensure that there are no typos or spelling errors. Take note of uppercase and lowercase letters, as your password is case-sensitive.

2. **Account Lockouts:** If you have attempted multiple login attempts with incorrect information, your account may get locked for security reasons. In such cases, you should wait for a specified period, usually a few minutes, before attempting to log in again.

3. **Browser Compatibility:** Ensure that you are using an updated web browser that is compatible with the 360 Promotions website. Clear your browser cache and cookies if you encounter any technical glitches.
