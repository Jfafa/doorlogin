## How to 365 Login UCL

### Introduction

If you are a student at University College London (UCL) or an employee of the institution, you may be looking for a guide on how to perform a 365 login for accessing various university services. This article aims to provide you with a detailed step-by-step process on how to successfully log in to your UCL 365 account. By following these instructions, you will be able to access the plethora of services offered by UCL, such as email, calendar, and online collaboration tools.

### Step 1: Open your preferred web browser

To begin the process of 365 login at UCL, launch your preferred web browser on your computer or mobile device. Whether you use Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Safari, or any other browser, the login procedure remains the same.

### Step 2: Visit the UCL homepage

Type in the official UCL website address in the URL bar of your web browser and hit the Enter key. Once the UCL homepage loads, you will have access to various resources, news, and information about different university departments and services.

### Step 3: Locate the "Login" button

On the homepage, navigate to the top right corner of the website. Look for a button labeled "Login" or "Sign in" and click on it. This will take you to the login page where you can enter your credentials to access your UCL 365 account.

### Step 4: Enter your UCL credentials

On the login page, you will be prompted to enter your UCL userID and password. Make sure you have these details handy to proceed. Carefully type in your userID and password in the respective fields provided.

### Step 5: Verify your login

After entering your UCL credentials, double-check the accuracy of the information you provided. Make sure there are no typos or mistakes. Once you are confident that the information is correct, click on the "Login" button to verify your credentials and access your UCL 365 account.

### Step 6: Explore UCL 365 services

Congratulations! You have successfully completed the 365 login process for UCL. Now you can explore the wide range of services offered through your UCL 365 account. Some of the key services you can access include:

- **Email**: UCL provides a reliable and secure email system for students and staff. You can send and receive emails, manage folders, and utilize various productivity features.

- **Calendar**: Stay organized by utilizing the calendar feature within UCL 365. Schedule appointments, meetings, and set reminders to ensure you don't miss any important events.

- **OneDrive**: UCL offers cloud storage through OneDrive, allowing you to securely store, share, and collaborate on documents, presentations, and other files.

- **Microsoft Teams**: Collaborate with fellow students or colleagues through Microsoft Teams. This platform allows for easy communication, file sharing, and project management.

- **Office Apps**: With UCL 365, you have access to a suite of Microsoft Office applications such as Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and more. Create, edit, and share documents seamlessly.

These are just a few examples of the extensive services available within your UCL 365 account. Take some time to explore and make full use of these resources to enhance your academic or professional experience at UCL.

### Frequently Asked Questions

#### Q1: Can I access my UCL 365 account from any device?
A1: Yes, you can access your UCL 365 account from any device with an internet connection. Whether you are using a computer, smartphone, or tablet, simply open a web browser and follow the login process mentioned in this article.

#### Q2: What should I do if I forget my UCL userID or password?
A2: In case you forget your UCL userID or password, visit the UCL website and locate the login page. Click on the "Forgot UserID" or "Forgot Password" link, and follow the instructions provided to recover your credentials. Alternatively, you can contact the UCL IT Support team for further assistance.

#### Q3: Can I customize the appearance of my UCL 365 account?
A3: Yes, UCL 365 offers various customization options. Once you have logged in, you can personalize the layout, theme, and display settings according to your preferences. Explore the settings menu to find these customization options and make your UCL 365 account feel truly personalized.

### Conclusion

Logging in to your UCL 365 account is a straightforward process that grants you access to numerous services and resources essential for your academic or professional journey at University College London. Remember to keep your credentials secure and explore the various tools available within your UCL 365 account to make the most of your experience at UCL.
