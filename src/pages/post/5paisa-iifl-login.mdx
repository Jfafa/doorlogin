Once you have successfully signed up, you can proceed to the login process.

## 3. Logging in to your 5paisa IIFL Account

1. Go to the official 5paisa website.

### Q1. Can I use my 5paisa login credentials for IIFL login as well?

No, the login credentials for 5paisa and IIFL are different. While both platforms are associated, they maintain separate login systems. So, you need to use your 5paisa login credentials for 5paisa and IIFL login credentials for IIFL.

### Q2. What should I do if I forget my 5paisa IIFL login password?

In case you forget your 5paisa IIFL account password, you can easily reset it by following these steps:

1. Go to the login page of either 5paisa or IIFL, depending on which account's password you have forgotten.
2. Look for the 'Forgot Password' or 'Reset Password' option and click on it.
3. Enter your registered email address or mobile number.
4. You will receive an OTP (One-Time Password) on your registered contact details.
5. Provide the OTP in the respective field as prompted.

7. Click on the 'Reset' or 'Change Password' button to complete the process.
