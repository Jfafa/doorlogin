## How to 365 Login UC Davis

If you are a student or faculty member at UC Davis, you will likely need to access various resources and tools provided by the university online. One such tool is the 365 Login portal, which gives you access to email, documents, and other Microsoft Office 365 applications. In this article, we will provide a step-by-step guide on how to 365 login UC Davis, ensuring a smooth and hassle-free process.

### Step 1: Open a Web Browser

To begin, open your preferred web browser on your device. This could be Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Safari, or any other browser of your choice. Ensure that you have a stable internet connection to avoid any interruptions during the login process.

### Step 2: Visit the UC Davis Website

Next, navigate to the official website of UC Davis by typing "ucdavis.edu" in the address bar of your web browser. Press Enter or Return to proceed.

### Step 3: Locate the 365 Login Page

Once on the UC Davis website, search for the login page specifically for Microsoft Office 365. This page will allow you to access your email, documents, and other Office 365 applications. You can usually find this login page in the top right corner of the website, labeled as "365 Login" or "Office 365."

### Step 4: Click on the 365 Login Link

Click on the provided link to access the 365 Login page. This will redirect you to the Microsoft Office 365 login portal.

### Step 5: Enter Your UC Davis Email Address

On the Microsoft Office 365 login page, you will see a field to enter your email address. Ensure that you enter your full UC Davis email address correctly. This typically follows the format "yourusername@ucdavis.edu."

### Step 6: Click on "Next"

After entering your UC Davis email address, click on the "Next" button to proceed to the next step.

### Step 7: Enter Your UC Davis Kerberos Passphrase

In the next screen, you will be prompted to enter your UC Davis Kerberos passphrase. This is the password associated with your UC Davis email account. Enter your passphrase carefully, ensuring that you input the correct characters.

### Step 8: Click on "Sign In"

Once you have entered your UC Davis Kerberos passphrase, click on the "Sign In" button to proceed.

### Step 9: Access 365 Services

Congratulations! You have successfully logged in to your UC Davis 365 account. You can now access various services provided by Microsoft Office 365, including Outlook for email, OneDrive for storing and sharing documents, Teams for collaboration, and many more.
