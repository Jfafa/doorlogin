## Employee Login Process at 3i Infotech
Follow the steps below to successfully log into your 3i Infotech employee account:

### Step 1: Open a Web Browser
Launch your preferred web browser on your desktop or mobile device. Ensure that you have a stable internet connection.

### Step 2: Visit the 3i Infotech Employee Login Page
Type the following URL into the address bar: `https://employeeportal.3i-infotech.com`. Hit Enter to load the 3i Infotech employee login page.

### Step 3: Enter your User Credentials
On the login page, you will find a login form. Enter your unique employee ID and password in the appropriate fields. Make sure to double-check the information for accuracy.

### Step 4: Click on the "Login" Button
After entering your credentials, click on the "Login" button to proceed.

### Step 5: Access your Employee Account
If the provided credentials are correct, you will be granted access to your 3i Infotech employee account. You can now explore and utilize all the features and resources offered within the portal.

## Troubleshooting Login Issues
Encountering login problems can be frustrating, but don't worry! We've got you covered with some potential solutions to common login issues at 3i Infotech:

### 1. Forgotten Password
If you forget your password, follow these steps to reset it:

1. On the 3i Infotech employee login page, click on the "Forgot Password" link.
2. You will be redirected to the password recovery page.
3. Enter your registered email address and click on the "Submit" button.
4. Check your email inbox for a password reset link.
5. Follow the instructions provided in the email to reset your password.
6. Once your password is reset, return to the login page and enter the new password to access your account.

### 2. Invalid Employee ID
If you receive an "invalid employee ID" error, ensure that you are entering the correct ID. If you are still facing issues, reach out to your HR department for assistance. They will be able to verify your employee ID and provide further guidance.

### 3. Account Lockout
In case your account gets locked due to multiple unsuccessful login attempts, you can try the following steps:

1. Contact your HR department or IT support to notify them about the situation.
2. Provide them with your employee ID and explain the account lockout issue.
3. They will assist you in unlocking your account or guide you through the necessary steps to regain access.

Remember, if you are experiencing any login-related problems, it is crucial to contact the appropriate support channels within 3i Infotech.
