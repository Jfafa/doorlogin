Now that you have reached the login page, follow the subsequent steps to log into your 123Landlord account:

### Step 1: Enter Your Username

On the login page, locate the field designated for entering your username. Click on the field and type in the username associated with your 123Landlord account. Take care to enter it accurately, paying attention to capital letters, numbers, and special characters, if applicable.

Directly below the username field, you will find a password field. Click on this field and enter your corresponding password. Ensure that you accurately input your password by being mindful of capitalization, special characters, and the correct sequence of lowercase and uppercase letters.
