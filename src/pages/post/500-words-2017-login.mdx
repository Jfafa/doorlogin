## How to 500 Words 2017 Login

In this article, we will guide you on how to login to the 500 Words 2017 platform. 500 Words is an annual writing competition for children, held by BBC Radio 2. It encourages young writers to showcase their talent and creativity by submitting a story of up to 500 words. To participate and login to the 500 Words 2017 platform, follow the steps below.

### Step 1: Open the 500 Words 2017 Website

Start by opening your preferred web browser and typing in the URL for the 500 Words 2017 website. You can do this by entering "500words.co.uk" into the address bar and pressing Enter. This will take you to the official login page for the competition.

### Step 2: Locate the Login Section

Once you are on the 500 Words 2017 website, look for the login section. It is usually located at the top right-hand corner of the page. The login section is clearly labeled and often includes fields for you to enter your username or email address and password.

### Step 3: Enter Your Credentials

After locating the login section, enter your credentials into the appropriate fields. If you are a returning user, you will need to enter the username or email address associated with your account and the corresponding password. If you are a new user, you may need to create an account first by clicking on the "Register" or "Sign Up" button, which is usually located near the login section.

### Step 4: Click the Login Button

Once you have entered your credentials, double-check them to ensure they are accurate. Then, click on the "Login" button to proceed. The platform will authenticate your information and redirect you to your account homepage or dashboard.

### Frequently Asked Questions

#### Q1: How do I recover my forgotten password?

A1: If you have forgotten your password, don't worry! Simply click on the "Forgot Password" link located near the login section. You will be prompted to enter the email address associated with your account. After submitting the form, check your email inbox for further instructions on how to reset your password.

#### Q2: Is there a mobile app for the 500 Words 2017 competition?

A2: Unfortunately, there is no dedicated mobile app for the 500 Words 2017 competition. However, the website is mobile-friendly, meaning you can access and login to your account using your smartphone or tablet's web browser.

#### Q3: Can I edit my submitted story after logging in?

A3: No, once you have submitted your story, you cannot edit it through the login portal. Therefore, it is crucial to ensure that your story is complete and accurate before submitting it to the competition. Take your time to review and proofread your work before final submission.

By following the steps outlined above, you can easily access your account and participate in this exciting writing competition for children. Remember to keep your login credentials secure and double-check the information you enter to ensure a successful login experience.
