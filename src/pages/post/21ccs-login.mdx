### Step 3: Clicking on the Login Button
Once you have located the login button, click on it to proceed with the login process. This action will typically direct you to the login page where you can enter your username and password.

### Step 4: Entering Your Username and Password
On the login page, you will find input fields to enter your username and password. Carefully type in your registered username and password in the respective boxes. Be cautious while entering the information to avoid any typographical errors.

### Step 5: Verifying Your Credentials
After entering your credentials, double-check the details to ensure accuracy. Make sure your username and password are entered correctly, with no extra spaces or misspellings. Verifying your credentials is crucial as incorrect login information may result in an authentication failure.

### Step 6: Submitting Your Login Information
Once you have confirmed the accuracy of your username and password, it's time to submit your login information. Look for a button labeled "Submit," "Login," or similar, and click on it to proceed.

### Step 7: Authenticating and Accessing Your Account
After submitting your login information, the system will verify your credentials against the stored data. If your login details match what is recorded in the system, you will be granted access to your 21CCS account. Congratulations! You have successfully logged in to 21CCS.
