Once you have successfully logged in to 4DX, you may be presented with options to choose your desired access level. After logging in to 4DX, you will be directed to the dashboard. The dashboard serves as the central hub for accessing key features and functionalities. ### Step 6: Start Utilizing the 4DX Features
