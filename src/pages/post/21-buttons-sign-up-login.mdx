## How to Sign Up and Login to 21 Buttons

### Introduction
If you are interested in fashion and want to discover new styles, 21 Buttons is the perfect platform for you. This popular social network allows users to share their fashion choices, follow influencers, and shop directly from their favorite looks. Signing up and logging into 21 Buttons is quick and easy. In this article, we will guide you through the process in detail, ensuring a seamless experience. 

### Signing Up for 21 Buttons
1. Go to the 21 Buttons website.
2. Click on the "Sign Up" button located at the top right corner of the homepage.
3. You will be presented with two options: signing up with your email or with your existing Facebook account. Choose the option that suits you best.
4. If you select the email option, enter your email address in the designated field. Make sure to use a valid email address as you will receive a verification email.
5. Create a strong password and enter it in the password field. It is recommended to use a combination of lowercase and uppercase letters, numbers, and special characters. Ensure your password is secure yet easy for you to remember.
6. Click on the "Sign Up" button to proceed.
7. If you chose to sign up using your Facebook account, click on the respective button and follow the prompts to authorize 21 Buttons to access your Facebook details.

### Logging into 21 Buttons
1. Open the 21 Buttons website on your preferred browser.
2. Click on the "Log in" button at the top right corner of the homepage.
3. Enter either your registered email address or username in the designated field. If you signed up using your Facebook account, you can simply click on the "Continue with Facebook" button to log in.
4. Input your password in the password field. Ensure that you enter the correct password associated with your 21 Buttons account.
5. After verifying that the information entered is accurate, click on the "Log In" button.
6. If you have forgotten your password, you can click on the "Forgot Password?" link and follow the necessary steps to reset it.
