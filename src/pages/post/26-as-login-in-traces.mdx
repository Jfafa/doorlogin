## How to 26 as Login in Traces

### Introduction

If you are looking for information on how to `26 as login in Traces`, you have come to the right place. Traces (TDS Reconciliation Analysis and Correction Enabling System) is a web-based application launched by the Income Tax Department of India for efficient administration and management of Tax Deducted at Source (TDS). In this article, we will explore step-by-step instructions on how to login to Traces and access the necessary features and functions it offers.

### Logging in to Traces

To access all the functionalities of Traces, a user needs to login using their registered User ID, Password, Tax Deduction and Collection Account Number (TAN), and the verification code displayed on the login page. Follow the below steps to log in successfully:

#### Step 1: Visit the Traces Website

Open your preferred web browser and visit the official Traces website by typing `www.tdscpc.gov.in` in the address bar. Hit Enter, and the Traces homepage will load.

#### Step 2: Click on "Login to TRACES"

On the Traces homepage, you will find the option "Login to TRACES" in the top-right corner of the page. Click on it to proceed to the login screen.

#### Step 3: Fill in Login Credentials

Once you reach the login page, you need to provide your User ID, Password, TAN, and the displayed verification code in the respective input fields. Double-check the accuracy of the information entered.

#### Step 4: Click on "Login"

After entering the required details, click on the "Login" button to initiate the login process.

### Understanding Traces Dashboard

Upon successful login, you will be redirected to the Traces dashboard. The dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of various functionalities and options available within the system. It offers a user-friendly interface with easy navigation.

The Traces dashboard contains different sections, including:

1. **Profile**: This section allows you to manage and update your profile information, such as Name, PAN, Mobile Number, Email ID, etc.

2. **Statement/Payment**: Here, you can access and download various types of statements, such as TDS/TCS statements, Form 26AS, Consolidated File, and more.

3. **TDS Compliance**: This section enables you to analyze and rectify any discrepancies or errors in TDS filings and compliance.

4. **Defaults**: You can utilize this section to view and address any TDS defaults or outstanding demands that need attention.

5. **Others**: This section consists of miscellaneous options like registering a grievance, downloading utilities and add-ons, and more.

### Frequently Asked Questions

#### Q1: Can I access Traces without a registered User ID and TAN?

Ans: No, a valid and registered User ID, along with the respective Tax Deduction and Collection Account Number (TAN), is mandatory to login to Traces.

#### Q2: What should I do if I forget my Traces login password?

Ans: If you forget your Traces login password, you can click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Follow the on-screen instructions and provide the required information to recover or reset your password.

#### Q3: Are there any charges associated with using the Traces platform?

Ans: No, the Traces platform is free to use for all users. However, note that certain services or functionalities may have associated charges as per the policies of the Income Tax Department.
