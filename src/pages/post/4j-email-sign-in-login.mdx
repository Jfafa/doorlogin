Q2: Can I access my 4J email account from a mobile device?
A2: Yes, you can access your 4J email account from a mobile device. Ensure that you have a stable internet connection and follow the steps mentioned earlier using your mobile device's web browser. Q3: Are there any additional security measures I should take to protect my 4J email account?
A3: Absolutely! To enhance the security of your 4J email account, consider enabling two-factor authentication (2FA).
