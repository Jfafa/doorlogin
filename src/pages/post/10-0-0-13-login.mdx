### Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

#### Q1: What should I do if I forget my router's username and password?

A1: If you can't recall your router's login credentials, you can try resetting the device to its factory settings. Look for a reset button on the router, press and hold it for about 10 seconds using a paperclip or similar tool. This will reset the router to its initial configuration, including the default username and password.

#### Q2: How can I find the default username and password for my router?

A2: Usually, the default username and password for routers are either mentioned in the device's user manual or can be found on a sticker attached to the router itself. Additionally, you can search online for the default credentials associated with your router's make and model.

#### Q3: What if I am unable to access the router login page?

A3: Ensure that your device is connected to the correct network and try clearing your browser cache and cookies. If the issue persists, restart your device and the router. If you still cannot access the login page, refer to the router's documentation or contact the manufacturer's customer support for assistance.
