To initiate the personal banking login process, you need to visit the official website of your bank. Open your preferred web browser and enter the web address of your bank. Ensure that you are accessing the official website, as there may be fraudulent websites posing as your bank's login page.

Once you have reached the official website, navigate to the login section. Look for the prominent "Login" button or link on the homepage. The placement of the login section may vary depending on the bank's website design, but it is usually positioned at the top right corner of the page.

### Step 3: Enter Your Credentials

Click on the "Login" button, which will redirect you to the login page. Here, you will be prompted to enter your personal banking credentials. Provide your username or customer ID in the designated field. It is essential to ensure the accuracy of the information you enter to avoid any login issues.
