While the login process for 3M Infocenter is relatively straightforward, users may encounter some common issues. Here are a few scenarios you might face and their solutions:

If you forget your password, don't panic. Follow these steps to reset it:

- Click on the "Forgot Password" link below the login form.
- You will be redirected to the password reset page.
- Enter your registered email address or username.
- Check your email inbox for a password reset link.
- Follow the instructions provided to create a new password.
- Log in using your updated credentials.

#### 2. Account Lockout
In some cases, repeated failed login attempts may result in temporary account lockout. To resolve this issue:

- Wait for the lockout period to expire (usually a few minutes).
- Ensure you are entering the correct login credentials.
- If you're still unable to access your account, contact the 3M Infocenter support team for further assistance.

#### 3. Invalid Username or Email Address
If you are receiving an error message stating that your username or email address is invalid, check the following:

- Ensure there are no typos or extra spaces in the username/email field.
- Confirm that you are using the same email address or username you used during the registration process.
- If you have recently changed your email address and haven't updated it in your 3M Infocenter account, contact support to resolve the issue.
