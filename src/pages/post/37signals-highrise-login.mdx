## Steps to Login to 37signals Highrise

### Step 1: Access the Official Website
To begin your Highrise journey, open your preferred web browser and enter the official Highrise website's URL in the address bar. Make sure to type in the correct domain (e.g., highrisehq.com) to avoid any potential scam or phishing attempts.

### Step 2: Locate the Login Button
Once you land on the Highrise website's homepage, take a moment to familiarize yourself with the layout. Look for the login button, usually positioned prominently in the upper right corner of the page. The button might be labeled as "Login," "Sign In," or feature an icon resembling a key or a person.

### Step 3: Click on the Login Button
Click on the login button, and you will be redirected to the Highrise login page. Ensure that the website's URL in the address bar starts with "https://" instead of "http://" to ensure a secure connection.

### Step 4: Enter Your Credentials
On the Highrise login page, you will see two empty fields labeled "Email" and "Password." Enter the email address associated with your Highrise account in the first field. Double-check the email spelling to avoid any login issues. Next, carefully type your unique password in the second field. Note that passwords are case-sensitive, so ensure that you use the proper capitalization.

### Step 5: Complete the Login Process
After entering your email address and password, review the information you provided for accuracy. Once confirmed, click the "Login" button or press the enter key. Highrise will validate your credentials, and if correct, you will be logged in to your account.
