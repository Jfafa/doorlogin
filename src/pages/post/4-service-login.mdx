Once you have entered your credentials, move the cursor to the login button. This button is usually labeled as "Login," "Sign In," or may contain an icon like an arrow or a padlock. ### Top Tips for a Successful 4 Service Login

To enhance your login experience and ensure a smooth authentication process, consider the following tips:

1. **Remember Your Credentials**: It is crucial to securely store your username/email and password for future reference. Using a password manager or writing them down in a secure location can save you from unnecessary login troubles.

2. **Use Strong Passwords**: Create a unique and strong password by including a combination of lowercase and uppercase letters, numbers, and special characters. Avoid using easily guessable information, such as birthdates or names.
