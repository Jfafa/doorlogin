- Keep your 2U account secure: Since your 2U account essentially acts as a gateway to multiple accounts, it is crucial to ensure its security. Use a strong and unique password for your 2U account and consider enabling two-factor authentication for an additional layer of protection.
- Regularly update your linked accounts' passwords: Although the convenience of 2U One Login allows you to manage all your accounts easily, it is important to periodically update your passwords. This practice will further enhance the security of your online presence.
