Once you have provided your User ID, the next step is to enter your password. Your password should have been chosen by you during the registration process. It is important to treat your password with utmost confidentiality and never share it with anyone. Ensure you enter your password correctly, paying attention to uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters.

After you have entered both your User ID and password, double-check your inputs to ensure accuracy. Once you are confident that the information provided is correct, click on the "Login" button. This will initiate the process of logging in to your 121FCU account.
