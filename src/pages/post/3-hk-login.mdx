Once you are on the 3 HK website, look for the login section. Usually, it can be found at the top right-hand corner of the homepage. The login section typically consists of spaces to enter your username and password.

In the provided space, enter the username associated with your 3 HK account. Make sure to double-check your username for any typos to avoid login issues. If you have forgotten your password, you can click on the "Forgot Password" link to initiate the password recovery process.

Once you have entered your username and password correctly, it's time to log in. Locate the login button on the website, which is usually labeled as "Login" or "Sign In." Click on it to proceed.
