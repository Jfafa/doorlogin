# How to 1st GHS Login

## Introduction
In this article, we will guide you through the process of logging in to 1st GHS (Global Health Strategy) platform. 1st GHS is a comprehensive online system that enables healthcare organizations to manage their patient data, appointments, and billing information efficiently. Logging in to the platform is the first step toward accessing its various features and functionalities. We will outline the instructions in a step-by-step manner, ensuring that you can understand and execute the process effortlessly.

## Step-by-Step Guide to 1st GHS Login
Follow the steps below to log in to your 1st GHS account successfully:

### Step 1: Access the 1st GHS Login Page
To start the login process, open your preferred web browser and enter the URL of the 1st GHS platform. The login page should appear, displaying the necessary fields to enter your credentials.

### Step 2: Enter your Username
In the first empty field on the login page, input your username correctly. Make sure to double-check the username for any typing errors as it is case-sensitive.

### Step 3: Provide your Password
The next input field is dedicated to your password. Carefully enter your password, ensuring that you type it accurately. Pay attention to uppercase and lowercase letters, as the system is case-sensitive.

### Step 4: Click on the "Login" Button
Once you have entered your username and password, locate the "Login" button on the page. Give it a single click to initiate the login process.

### Step 5: Complete the Two-factor Authentication (If Applicable)
As an additional security measure, 1st GHS may require two-factor authentication (2FA). If enabled for your account, you will receive a verification code via your registered email address or mobile device. Enter the verification code in the designated field to complete the authentication process.

### Step 6: Access your 1st GHS Dashboard
Upon successful login, you will be directed to your personalized 1st GHS dashboard. From here, you can navigate through the various modules, such as patient management, appointment scheduling, and more.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q1: Can I reset my 1st GHS password if I forget it?
A1: Yes, you can reset your 1st GHS password if you forget it. Simply click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Follow the instructions provided to reset your password and regain access to your account.

### Q2: What if I encounter login issues even with the correct credentials?
A2: If you experience login issues despite entering the correct username and password, ensure that you have a stable internet connection. Clear your browser cache and cookies, then attempt to log in again. If the problem persists, contact the 1st GHS support team for further assistance.

### Q3: Can I access 1st GHS from a mobile device?
A3: Yes, you can access 1st GHS from a mobile device. The platform is designed to be mobile-responsive, allowing you to conveniently log in and access your account from smartphones or tablets. Simply open your mobile browser and follow the same login steps outlined above.

By following this comprehensive guide, you should now be able to successfully log in to your 1st GHS account. Remember to keep your login credentials secure and notify 1st GHS support immediately if you encounter any suspicious activities or issues during the login process.
