To ensure a seamless login process, consider the following tips:

- **Remember Your Login Credentials:** It's essential to memorize your username and password or keep them stored securely. This will save you time and effort when accessing your school account in the future.

- **Clear Browser Cache:** Periodically clearing your browser cache helps improve performance and resolves any potential login issues. This ensures you have a smooth experience whenever you attempt to log in.

- **Update Your Password Regularly:** As a security measure, it is advisable to update your password at regular intervals. This decreases the chances of unauthorized access and protects your personal information.
