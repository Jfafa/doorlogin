On the registration page, you will be asked to provide essential information to create your 000webhost account. The details typically include:

- **Email Address**: Enter a valid email address that you have access to as it will be used for future correspondence.

After entering your details, click on the "Get Started" or "Sign Up" button to proceed. You will receive an email from 000webhost with a verification link. Click on the link to verify your email address and activate your account.
