To start the login process, open your preferred web browser and navigate to the official 123 and Me website. You can do this by typing "123 and Me" into your search engine or directly entering www.123andme.com into the address bar.

Once you are on the 123 and Me homepage, carefully scan the page until you find the "Login" button. It is usually located in the top right corner of the website header. It might be helpful to look for a blue button with the word "Login" or an icon resembling a key.

### Step 3: Click on the Login Button

When you have found the "Login" button, click on it to proceed to the login page. This action will typically redirect you to a new page where you can enter your login credentials.

### Step 4: Enter Your Email and Password

On the login page, you will be prompted to enter your registered email address and password. Type in the email address associated with your 123 and Me account in the designated field. Make sure to input it accurately to avoid any errors. Then, move on to the password field and enter your unique password. Double-check for any typos or spelling mistakes.

### Step 5: Complete the CAPTCHA

To ensure the security of your account, 123 and Me may employ a CAPTCHA verification process. CAPTCHA stands for "Completely Automated Public Turing test to tell Computers and Humans Apart." You may be asked to solve a simple puzzle, select specific images, or enter a series of characters. Follow the instructions on the screen and complete the CAPTCHA to verify that you are a real person accessing the account.

### Step 6: Click on the Login Button

After you have filled in your email address, password, and successfully solved the CAPTCHA, look for the "Login" button on the page. It is typically positioned beneath or next to the input fields. Click on it to initiate the login process.

#### Q2: Can I use my 123 and Me login credentials on the mobile app as well?
