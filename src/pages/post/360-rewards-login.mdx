5. **Click on the "Login" Button**: Once you have entered your username and password, click on the "Login" button located below the input fields.
6. **Access Your Account**: After successfully completing the above steps, you will be redirected to your 360 Rewards account dashboard, where you can explore and manage your rewards and benefits.

2. **Open the 360 Rewards App**: Once the app is successfully installed, locate and tap on its icon from your device's home screen or app drawer to launch it.
3. **Enter Your Credentials**: On the login screen of the app, enter your username and password in the appropriate fields. Double-check that you have entered the correct information.
4. **Tap on "Login"**: After ensuring the accuracy of your login credentials, tap on the "Login" button, usually located below the input fields.

A1: Yes, you can easily reset your 360 Rewards account password in case you forget it. Simply visit the login page and locate the "Forgot password" option. ### Q2: What should I do if I encounter an "Invalid username or password" error during the login process?

A2: If you receive an error indicating an invalid username or password, ensure that you have entered your login credentials correctly.
