# How to 123 Reg Webmail Login

Are you looking for a step-by-step guide on how to login to the 123 Reg Webmail? You have come to the right place. In this article, we will provide you with a detailed walkthrough of the login process, along with helpful tips and information to make your experience seamless.

## Before you begin

Before we dive into the login process, it's important to ensure that you have the necessary credentials to access your 123 Reg Webmail. Make sure you have your email address and password handy as you will need them during the login process.

## Step 1: Accessing the 123 Reg Webmail login page

To begin the login process, you need to visit the official 123 Reg website. Open your preferred web browser and type in "www.123-reg.co.uk" in the address bar. Once the page is loaded, you can proceed to the next step.

## Step 2: Finding the Webmail login option

On the 123 Reg website, navigate to the top-right corner of the page. Look for the "Login" button and click on it. This will open a drop-down menu with various login options. From the list, select "Webmail Login" and click on it. You will be redirected to the Webmail login page.

## Step 3: Entering your email address and password

On the Webmail login page, you will find two empty fields: one for your email address and the other for your password. Fill in the respective fields with the correct information. Ensure that you enter the complete email address, including the domain name (e.g., yourname@yourdomain.com).

## Step 4: Logging in to your 123 Reg Webmail account

After entering the required details, it's time to log in to your 123 Reg Webmail account. Double-check the information you have entered to avoid any typing errors. Once you are confident that everything is correct, click on the "Log In" button.

Congratulations! If you followed the steps correctly, you should now be logged in to your 123 Reg Webmail account. You will be directed to the Webmail interface, where you can access and manage your emails.

## Tips for a successful login

- Always ensure that you have a stable internet connection before attempting to login.
- Double-check your email address and password for accuracy before logging in.
- If you tend to forget your password, consider using a password manager to securely store and retrieve your login credentials.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q1: I forgot my password. What should I do?

A1: If you forget your password, don't worry. On the Webmail login page, below the password field, you will find a link titled "Forgot your password?". Click on it, and you will be prompted to enter your email address. Follow the instructions provided to reset your password.

### Q2: Can I access 123 Reg Webmail on mobile devices?

A2: Yes, you can access your 123 Reg Webmail account on mobile devices. The Webmail interface is mobile-friendly and can be accessed using a web browser on your smartphone or tablet.

### Q3: How do I set up automatic email forwarding in 123 Reg Webmail?

A3: To set up automatic email forwarding in 123 Reg Webmail, log in to your account and navigate to the settings or preferences section. Look for the option related to email forwarding and follow the prompts to configure the desired forwarding settings.
