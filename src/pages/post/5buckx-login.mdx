## Troubleshooting 5Buckx Login Issues

### Forgot Password

If you have forgotten your password, don't worry, as 5Buckx provides a simple way to reset it. Follow the steps below:

1. Visit the 5Buckx login page.

2. Click on the "Forgot Password" link, usually located near the login fields.

3. Enter the email address associated with your 5Buckx account.

4. Click on the "Submit" or similar button.

5. Check your email inbox for a password reset link from 5Buckx.

6. Click on the password reset link provided in the email.

7. Follow the instructions on the password reset page to create a new password.

### Account Locked

If your 5Buckx account is locked due to multiple unsuccessful login attempts or for any other reason, you need to take the following steps to regain access:

1. Visit the 5Buckx login page.

2. Click on the "Account Locked" or "Unlock Account" link provided near the login fields.

3. You might be prompted to verify your identity through additional security measures such as solving a captcha or receiving a verification code.

4. Follow the on-screen instructions to successfully unlock your account.

### Invalid Credentials

If you are consistently getting an "Invalid Credentials" error while attempting to log in, ensure that you are entering the correct email address or username and password combination. If you are still facing issues, the following tips might help:

1. Double-check your email address or username for any typos.

2. Ensure that the Caps Lock key is not accidentally enabled on your keyboard.

3. Try entering your password slowly and carefully to avoid any input errors.

4. If you are confident in the accuracy of your login credentials, try resetting your password using the "Forgot Password" option.
