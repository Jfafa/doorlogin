## How to 1st Advantage Login

### Step 1: Open the 1st Advantage Website
To initiate the login process, you need to open the official website of 1st Advantage in your preferred web browser. Simply type "1st Advantage" into the search bar and click on the official website link from the search results.

### Step 2: Locate the Login Area
Once you are on the 1st Advantage homepage, navigate your way to the login area. You can typically find the login area towards the top right corner of the webpage. Look for a section titled "Login" or "Account Login."

### Step 3: Enter your Username
In the login area, you will be prompted to enter your username. This is the unique identifier associated with your 1st Advantage Banking account. Carefully enter your username in the designated field. Make sure to double-check for any typos or errors to avoid login issues.

### Step 4: Enter your Password
After entering your username, the next step is to enter your password. Your password serves as a security measure to ensure that only authorized users can access your account. Take caution while entering your password to ensure the correct characters are inputted.

### Step 5: Select your Login Type
1st Advantage provides different login types based on the services you need. Choose the appropriate login type from the provided options. This could be Personal Online Banking, Business Online Banking, or any other specialized services you might have with 1st Advantage.

### Step 6: Click on "Login"
Once you have entered your username, password, and selected the appropriate login type, simply click on the "Login" button. This will initiate the login process and redirect you to your 1st Advantage account dashboard.
