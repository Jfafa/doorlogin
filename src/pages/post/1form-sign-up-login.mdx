# How to 1form Sign Up and Login?

1form is an online platform that simplifies the process of renting a property. It allows tenants to create a comprehensive rental application and submit it to multiple real estate agents with just one form. In this article, we will guide you through the step-by-step process of signing up and logging in to 1form, ensuring a smooth rental application experience.

## Signing Up on 1form

To get started with 1form, you need to create an account by following these simple steps:

1. Visit the official website of 1form.
2. Look for the "Sign Up" button on the homepage.
3. Click on the "Sign Up" button to proceed.
4. You will be redirected to the registration page.
5. Fill in the required information, including your name, email address, and password.
6. Review the terms and conditions before accepting them.
7. Complete any additional verification steps, such as solving a captcha or confirming your email address.
8. Once you have successfully registered, you will receive a confirmation email.
9. Click on the confirmation link provided in the email to activate your account.

Congratulations! You have now successfully signed up on 1form.

## Logging In to 1form

Now that you have an account on 1form, you can easily log in by following these steps:

1. Go to the 1form website.
2. Look for the "Log In" button, typically located at the top right corner of the homepage.
3. Click on the "Log In" button to proceed.
4. You will be redirected to the login page.
5. Enter your registered email address and password into the respective fields.
6. Make sure the information you entered is accurate and free of any errors.
7. If you forgot your password, there is usually an option to reset it by clicking on "Forgot Password" and following the guided instructions.
8. Once you have entered your credentials correctly, click on the "Log In" button.
9. If the provided information is valid, you will be successfully logged in to your 1form account.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q1: Can I edit my 1form rental application after submitting it?

A1: No, once you have submitted your 1form rental application, it cannot be edited. However, you can withdraw your application and submit a new one if necessary. To withdraw your application, log in to your 1form account, go to the Applications section, find the relevant application, and select the "Withdraw" option.

### Q2: Does 1form charge any fees for tenants?

A2: No, 1form is a free service for tenants. There are no hidden fees or charges associated with creating an account, submitting rental applications, or using any features available on the platform.

### Q3: How can I track the status of my rental applications on 1form?

A3: To track the status of your rental applications on 1form, log in to your account and navigate to the Applications section. Here, you will find a list of your submitted applications with their current statuses. The real estate agents will usually update these statuses to keep you informed throughout the application process.
