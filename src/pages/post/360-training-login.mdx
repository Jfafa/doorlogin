# How to 360 Training Login

## Introduction

In this comprehensive guide, we will walk you through the process of logging in to your 360 Training account. 360 Training is a leading provider of online training courses, offering a wide range of programs in various industries. Logging in to your account is essential to access and manage your courses, track your progress, and interact with course materials.

## Step-by-Step Process of 360 Training Login

Follow the step-by-step guide below to log in to your 360 Training account:

### Step 1: Access the 360 Training Website

First, open your preferred web browser and navigate to the official 360 Training website.

### Step 2: Locate the Login Button

Once on the website's homepage, look for the "Login" or "Sign In" button. You will usually find it at the top right corner of the webpage. Click on this button to proceed to the login page.

### Step 3: Enter Your Credentials

On the login page, you will be asked to provide your login credentials. These generally include your registered email address or username and your password. Ensure that you enter the correct information to avoid any login issues.

### Step 4: Click on the "Log In" Button

After entering your login credentials, double-check the information for accuracy. Once you are confident that you have provided the correct details, click on the "Log In" button to proceed.

### Step 5: Access Your Account Dashboard

If the login process is successful, you will be redirected to your 360 Training account dashboard. Here, you will have access to all the relevant features and functions associated with your selected courses and programs.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q1: What do I do if I forget my 360 Training password?

A1: In case you forget your password, don't worry! On the 360 Training login page, you will find a "Forgot Password?" option. Simply click on it, and the system will guide you through the password recovery process. You may need to provide the email address associated with your account to receive instructions on resetting your password.

### Q2: Can I change my email address associated with the 360 Training account?

A2: Yes, you can change the email address associated with your 360 Training account. To do so, log in to your account and navigate to the account settings or profile section. Here, you should find an option to update your email address. Follow the instructions provided, and make sure to verify the new email address through the confirmation link sent to your inbox.

### Q3: How can I enroll in new courses on 360 Training?

A3: To enroll in new courses on 360 Training, you need to log in to your account and navigate to the course catalog or search for specific courses using the search bar. Once you find the desired course, click on it to access the course details page. Here, you will typically find an "Enroll" or "Add to Cart" button. Click on it, and follow the prompts to complete the enrollment process.

## Conclusion

By following the step-by-step login process outlined in this article, you should easily be able to access your 360 Training account. Remember to enter your credentials accurately and keep them secure to maintain the integrity of your account. In case of any difficulties, consult the 360 Training support team for further assistance. Happy learning!
