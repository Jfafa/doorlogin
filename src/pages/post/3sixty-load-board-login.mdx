## How to 3sixty Load Board Login

### Step 1: Visit the 3sixty Load Board Login Page

To begin the login process, open your web browser and navigate to the official 3sixty Load Board website. Once there, locate and click on the login button, which is typically found at the top right corner of the homepage. This will redirect you to the login page where you can enter your credentials.

### Step 2: Enter Your Account Credentials

On the 3sixty Load Board login page, you will be prompted to enter your account credentials. Start by entering your registered email address in the designated field. Ensure that the email address you provide is associated with the account you wish to login to. Once you have entered your email address, proceed to the next step.

### Step 3: Provide Your Password

In the password field, enter the password that corresponds to your 3sixty Load Board account. It is crucial to accurately enter your password to ensure successful login. Take care to use the appropriate uppercase and lowercase letters, special characters, and numbers included in your password to avoid any authentication errors. Once you have entered your password, proceed to the next step.

### Step 4: Click on the "Login" Button

After entering your email address and password, double-check that you have entered the correct information. Once you are certain that your account credentials are accurate, click on the "Login" button. This will initiate the login process and verify your credentials with the 3sixty Load Board server.

### Step 5: Access Your 3sixty Load Board Account

Upon successful authentication of your credentials, you will be granted access to your 3sixty Load Board account. From here, you can take advantage of the various tools and features available on the platform to effectively manage your shipments, search for available loads, and connect with potential partners in the trucking industry.
