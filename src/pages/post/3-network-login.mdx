- Enter your email address or phone number registered with your 3 network account.
- Follow the instructions provided in the password reset email or SMS you receive.
- Create a new password and confirm it.
- Use this new password to log into your 3 network account.

#### 2. I'm unable to log into my 3 network account even with the correct credentials. What could be the issue?

If you're experiencing trouble logging in despite entering the correct credentials, try the following steps:

- Clear your browser cache and cookies to eliminate any temporary data conflicts.
- Try using a different web browser to rule out any browser-specific issues.
- If the problem persists, contact 3 network customer support for further assistance.
