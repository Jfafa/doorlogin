7. Finally, click on the "Sign Up" button to complete the registration process.

4. Type in your password securely.
5. Double-check the accuracy of the provided login credentials.

- No, to sign up for a 2 Degrees account, you will need a valid New Zealand mobile number. This number is required for verification purposes and to provide you with the best telecommunications services.
