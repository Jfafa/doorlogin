#### Step 4: Enter Your Login Credentials
On the 3DSky login page, you will find a form asking for your login credentials. Fill in the required fields with your registered email address and password. Make sure the information you enter is accurate to avoid any login issues.

After entering your login credentials correctly, click on the login or submit button to complete the login process. If your credentials are valid, you will be successfully logged into your 3DSky account.
