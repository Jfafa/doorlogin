### Step-by-Step Guide to Logging In

Now, let's dive into the step-by-step process to successfully log in to your 3 Mobile WiFi device:

#### Step 1: Connect to Your Mobile WiFi

Ensure that your device is powered on and within the range of the wireless network of your 3 Mobile WiFi device. To establish a connection, go to the available WiFi networks on your device and select the network name associated with your 3 Mobile WiFi device. Enter the required password if prompted, and wait for the connection to be established.

#### Step 2: Open a Web Browser

Once you are connected to the mobile WiFi network, open any web browser of your choice. Popular options include Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, or Safari.

#### Step 3: Access the Admin Interface

In the address bar of the web browser, type the IP address specific to your 3 Mobile WiFi device. Typically, this IP address is 192.168.0.1 or 192.168.1.1. Press Enter to proceed.

#### Step 4: Enter Login Credentials

After accessing the IP address in the browser, a login page will appear. Here, you need to enter the default username and password. The default credentials are usually mentioned in the instruction manual or can be found on the back of your mobile WiFi device. Enter the username and password correctly to proceed to the next step.
